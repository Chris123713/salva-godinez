========================================
  QUICK START
========================================

1. Type: /eb
2. Click: "Start New Elevator"
3. Walk to each floor and press E
4. Click: "Save Elevator"
5. Done!

========================================
  CONTROLS
========================================

/eb       - Toggle builder
F5        - Toggle UI visibility
E         - Add floor at position
Z         - Remove last floor
Click     - Teleport to floor
