Builder Help

Getting Started

  1. Click "Start New Elevator" or press E
  2. Enter a name for your elevator
  3. Walk to each floor location
  4. Press E or click "Add Floor Here" at each spot
  5. Click "Save Elevator" when done (min. 2 floors)

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • F5 - Toggle Builder UI
  • E - Add floor at current position
  • Z - Remove last floor

Tips

  • Visual markers show where floors are placed
  • You can move around freely while the UI is open
  • Click on floors in the list to teleport or edit them
  • Elevators need at least 2 floors to be saved
