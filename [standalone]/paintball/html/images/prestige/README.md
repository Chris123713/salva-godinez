# Prestige Icons

This folder contains the prestige icon images for the paintball system.

## Required Images

You need to create 6 prestige icon images (prestige_0.png through prestige_5.png):

### Prestige 0 - Recruit
- **Design**: A simple bronze or copper shield with a basic target/bullseye emblem
- **Style**: Metallic bronze with subtle glow, simple and clean design
- **File**: `prestige_0.png`

### Prestige 1 - Veteran  
- **Design**: A silver shield with a single star in the center, surrounded by subtle rays
- **Style**: Polished silver with golden accents, more detailed than prestige 0
- **File**: `prestige_1.png`

### Prestige 2 - Elite
- **Design**: A gold shield with crossed paintball markers or crossed weapons
- **Style**: Rich gold with intricate details, combat-focused design
- **File**: `prestige_2.png`

### Prestige 3 - Legend
- **Design**: A platinum shield with a stylized flame or phoenix emblem
- **Style**: Platinum/metallic with fire effects, dynamic and powerful
- **File**: `prestige_3.png`

### Prestige 4 - Master
- **Design**: A diamond/platinum shield with a lightning bolt or energy symbol
- **Style**: Brilliant platinum with electric effects, high-tech appearance
- **File**: `prestige_4.png`

### Prestige 5 - Grandmaster
- **Design**: A legendary shield with a crown or dragon emblem, most ornate design
- **Style**: Ultimate prestige - diamond/rainbow effects, most detailed and impressive
- **File**: `prestige_5.png`

## Image Specifications
- **Size**: 64x64 pixels (recommended) or 128x128 pixels for higher quality
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Style**: Metallic/shield-based design similar to Call of Duty prestige icons
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Color Scheme**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond progression

## Design Guidelines
- Each icon should be unique and visually distinct
- Progression should be obvious (each tier more impressive than the last)
- Maintain consistent shield/badge shape across all icons
- Use metallic textures and glow effects
- Keep file sizes reasonable (< 50KB per image recommended)
